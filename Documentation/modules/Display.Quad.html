Module Display.Quad

A Quad represents a rectangle with a uniform color or a color gradient.

It's possible to set one color per vertex. The colors will smoothly fade into each other over the area of the quad. To display a simple linear color gradient, assign one color to vertices 1 and 2 and another color to vertices 3 and 4.

if __USE_SIMULATION_COORDS__ is nil or false then vertex 1 is the top left one, and the vertices follow clockwise order.

if __USE_SIMULATION_COORDS__ is true then vertex 1 is the bottom left one, and the vertices follow counter clockwise order.
